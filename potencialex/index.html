THE MAIN FACTORS OF IMPOTENCES ARE

Psychological

Arousal starts from the top: a man should feel positive emotions, be calm and relaxed. The behavior of a woman is essential in the process of sexual arousal, sometimes an awkward word or excessive persistence on her part can significantly weaken an erection.

Hormone

A decrease in the production of male sex hormones usually occurs with age, but in some cases this phenomenon is due to a disease of the endocrine system. In recent years, doctors increasingly diagnose impotence in young obese men: excess adipose tissue changes the hormonal background of a person.

Neurological

Erectile dysfunction can be a consequence of disturbances in the functioning of the central or peripheral nervous system - after a previous illness or due to prolonged use of certain medications (antidepressants, beta-blockers, anti-allergy drugs, and others)

Vascular

Since an erection of the penis occurs due to the filling of its tissues with blood, vascular diseases such as atherosclerosis and hypertension can adversely affect erection. Often it is the vascular factor that causes impotence in men of mature age.
